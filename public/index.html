Coach's notes

"Strong knee bend and excellent balance through the push — your base is solid. The thing costing you speed is an upright torso on recovery; you're losing forward drive each stride. Fix the lean and the stride length follows."

▸Russian box drill — trains a lower, more forward body position under control.
▸Resisted starts — builds the forward lean and first-three-stride drive.
▸Single-leg glides — sharpens recovery timing between strides.
